About Me

Hi there! I'm Jonathan, a recent graduate from the University of Pennsylvania where I studied computer science (BSE) and physics, and am currently located in San Francisco, CA. I am interested in AI and machine learning, especially its applications in solving problems in games, computer vision, and finance. I am interested in research engineering positions that focus on these domains. If you're interested in or work in these areas, I'd love to chat ☺.

In my free time, I enjoy jamming with friends and having long talks about things I read. I am also interested in sleight of hand, and I do a bit of closeup card magic.

I think a lot about life — from science to philosophy, politcs to relationships — and I write about some of it in my blog. Thoughts always welcome!
