About Me

Simply the Best

A list of idols, mentors, and inspirations, unmatched in mental acuity, wisdom, or character. Gods among men and what I learned from them.

Randy Pausch — "If you lead your life correctly... your dreams will come to you". In the last lecture of his life, he talks about how to achieve your dreams and become a better person. His lecture has been the paradigm for my life principles.

Richard Feynman — A living example that teaching others is the only path to true understanding. The only person whose Nobel Prize is eclipsed by his teaching prowess and making physics universally accessible.

John von Neumann — A polymath whose name is staple in almost all fields of math and science. In my opinion, truly the smartest man in history.

Douglas MacArthur — "People of the Philippines, I have returned". A genuinely kind person, he kept an impossible promise during WWII. Instrumental in shaping Asia into what it is today. The first only western leader revered by the East.

Erwin Rommel — Incorruptible General, universally respected by his subordinates and adversaries. He showed that the ugliest jobs can be done with principles and honor.
